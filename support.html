Support the team

AUVIC’s projects and operations would not be possible without the support of our generous sponsors. By becoming a sponsor you will receive media coverage at all of the competitions we attend, and other public events the team participates in. You also have the opportunity to network with motivated and hardworking students at the University of Victoria.

Sponsorship includes square sticker space on the final submarine, on the team’s shirts, and on the AUVIC website to advertise your company. The submarine competes every year at the RoboSub competition in San Diego, California and is taken along for outreach events. The logo on the submarine will expose your company to the international AUV community, local communities, and -- universities who participate in the competition.
If you are interested in supporting AUVIC, email us at auvic@uvic.ca or you can support the team by donating at https://extrweb.uvic.ca/donate-online/auvic. Donations over $20 are eligible to receive a tax deductible charitable donation recept, as UVic is a registered charity.

Cheques should be made out to “UVic” with “AUVic” in the memo line and can be mailed directly to the address below.

Levels of Sponsorship

Our team requires support to travel to compeptitions, print banners, and designing our submarine. If your business provides items as a service you can also donate them and be counted as cash sponsorship. All sponsorship contributions will be provided with an acknowledgement receipt. We offer four levels of sponsorship, listed below:

Platinum ($5000+ in cash/equipment )
  • Large logo on website (and sidebar), vehicles, promotional materials
  • Invited to testing sessions and demo runs
Gold ($2500+ in cash/equipment )
  • Medium logo on website, vehicles, promotional materials
Silver ($1000+ in cash/equipment )
  • Slightly smaller logo on website, vehicles, promotional materials
Bronze ($500+ in cash/equipment )
  • Small logo on website
